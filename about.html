Who are we?

We are Breathe EZ Home Solutions. We got our start in 2014 after Assurance Heating & Air Conditioning wanted to provide air duct cleaning for our customers. Backed by the name and management of Assurance, Breathe EZ strives to give you the best service possible. Our professional service staff are trained in the best ways to clean ducts and always make sure to give great customer service while doing it.
Our Location
We service residential and commercial buildings in the northern Chicagoland area.
