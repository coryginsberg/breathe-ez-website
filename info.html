What is Air Duct Cleaning?

Duct cleaning generally refers to the cleaning of various heating and cooling system components of your home's central air. The most common way of doing this is by taking a powerful, specialized vacuum cleaner and directing it down your air vents to gather as much dust in the system as possible. Should I Get My Ducts Cleaned?

According to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), the average home accumulates about 40 pounds of dust per year. That's the average weight of a Border Collie! Imagine what that can do to you by breathing all that dust in. Cleaning your ducts not only cleans the air you breathe, but also helps people suffering from persistent allergies or asthma breathe better.

Before And After Duct Cleaning

You should clean your ducts if...

  • Your ducts are or were infested with varmin (e.g. rodents or insects)
  • Your ducts have noticable mold buildup
  • Particles or dust clumps are being discharges from your ducts
  • Allergies or asthma symptoms have become progressively worse when inside recently

What are some common causes of dirty ducts?

Allergies And Dust Dont Mix

Some of the most common causes of dirty ducts in a house include...

  • Pets
  • Dust
  • Renovations
  • DIY Projects
  • Cigarrette Smoke
  • Mold
